Singapore

We visited Singapore in 2013 in the month of March. We saw many places there like the Lego museum, Helix bridge, Santosa Island, Universal Studio, Marina Bay Sand Hotel and Garden By The Bay. They were amazing. One of the best things was the Lego museum. There were people, cars, and other various things made out of Lego blocks. It was a completely new experience.
